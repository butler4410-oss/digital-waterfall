Integration Capabilities

🔗

Pay Link Embedding

Unique Paymentus payment URLs automatically embedded in every SMS and email. Patients tap once to pay — no portal login, no app download.

⚡

Real-Time Payment Webhooks

When Paymentus confirms payment, a webhook instantly stops the waterfall cascade. No duplicate messages, no print for already-paid balances.

📈

Aging Data Sync

Outstanding balance aging from Paymentus drives priority tiers in the waterfall. Past-due 90+ days = Critical priority = fastest escalation path.

💰

BillWallet Integration

Patients who use Paymentus BillWallet get auto-enrolled for digital-first delivery. Stored payment methods enable one-tap payment from SMS.

🔒

Shared Compliance Layer

Both platforms are SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, and PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. Shared audit trail for every communication and payment event.

🛠

EHR Bridge

Paymentus connects to Epic MyChart and Oracle Health. Waterfall messages can deep-link patients directly into their MyChart payment view.
